As I'm sure you've noticed by now; this is a website developed by myself. But why? As a student of Computing at De Montfort University, I am expected to create many different types of programs and systems as part of the course syllabus. This site is a project undertaken as part of my Front End Web Development module; which the aim was to create an interactive, responsive website using the technologies we had learned prior to the assessment being given. Namely: HTML5, CSS and JavaScript.

As a third year Computing student, I have learnt many things over the years. From system design and development to writing research papers, from designing and developing websites to article comparison.
Where has this magic taken place?! At De Montfort University, of course. (Home of John'Totalbiscuit' Bain, for you eSports fans out there) Curious as to where it is? Here you are!

Web development technologies have come a long way in the past decade; hell, they've come a long way since I started with learning XHTML 3 years ago!
